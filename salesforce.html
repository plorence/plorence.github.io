Salesforce

At Salesforce, I focus exclusively on providing UX support for Internal Developer tools. Salesforce is a 26-year-old company with a monolithic codebase that has been slowly transitioning from private data centers to be delivered via public cloud infrastructure. In moving to the public cloud in a provider-agnostic way, Salesforce increases security, scalability and agility. This effort has been externally marketed as Hyperforce.

Our home-grown internal developer portal provides service owners insight into continuous integration (CI) and continuous delivery (CD) processes as well as other key views supporting the software development lifecycle. It is a tool somewhat analogous to GitLab or Backstage from Spotify. I designed multiple views in this portal, CLI commands, and myriad other views for specific personas like release managers or security compliance officers. These internal tools support more than 8,000 internal developers.
